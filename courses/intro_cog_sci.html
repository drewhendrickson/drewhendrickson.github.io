Introduction to Cognitive Science

This course is designed for first-year bachelor students in the Cognitive Science and Artificial Intelligence program at Tilburg University. Students in this course are required to be taking an Introduction to AI course in parallel, thus many AI topics have been omitted.

Textbook and readings

The textbook for the course is the first edition of Friedenberg and Silverman's Cognitive Science: An introduction to the mind. It is available here. Weekly readings from the journal Trends in Cognitive Science and similar outlets accompany each week's chapter from the textbook.
